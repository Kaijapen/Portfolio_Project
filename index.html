About Me ~
Hi there! Where to begin?
I grew up in a college town in NorthWest Arkansas, but moved out to the Bay Area in California when I was 11 years old. I have always had a nac for working with computers, and when it came to picking a major right out of high school I decided to give Computer Science a try! My first language was Java, which proved to be difficult to learn if you have never worked with code before. I persevered and fell in love with the art of coding. In June of 2022 I decided to enroll in a coding bootcamp called Coding Dojo, where I not only learned new skills, but also formed life long friendships and fell in love with teaching people how to code.
During my time in the bootcamp, I wanted to go above and beyond. I was determined to graduate with the highest pass I could get for each stack (MERN, Python, and C#). This meant getting a black belt score for each test. After achieving this, I had great recommendations from the instructors and teaching assistants that helped me throughout my journey, and I became a teaching assistant for Coding Dojo within a month of graduation.
Now, all I can hope for is that I keep learning more every single day, and hopefully achieve my dream of working with AI. <3
